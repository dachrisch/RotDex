# RotDex Economy & Progression Plan

## Current Economy State

### Resources
- **Energy**: Card generation resource (max 10, regenerates 1 per 30min)
- **Brainrot Coins**: Primary currency for gameplay actions
- **Gems**: Premium currency (future implementation)

### Current Costs
- **Card Generation**: 1 Energy + coins for extra characters (20 char free, then 1 coin per 10 chars)
- **Fusion**: 50 coins (non-refundable, chance-based success)
- **Basic Pack**: 100 coins (planned)
- **Premium Pack**: 50 gems (planned)

**Reward Pool** (weighted chances):
- 40% - Small Coins (50-100 coins)

### 5. Energy Regeneration
- Passive: 1 energy per 30 minutes (max 10)
